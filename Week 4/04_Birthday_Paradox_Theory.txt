# Birthday Paradox

## Concept
365 possible birthdays hone ke bawajood:
Sirf 23 logon me 50% chance hota hai ki do log same birthday share karein.

## Why It Happens?
Hum compare karte hain:
ANY pair with ANY pair.

Number of pairs:
n(n-1)/2

For 40 people:
40*39/2 = 780 comparisons

Isliye repetition jaldi hoti hai.

## Important Topics
- Probability
- Pigeonhole Principle
- Combinatorics
